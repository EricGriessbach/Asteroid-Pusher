COSMIC DELIVERY

MISSION BRIEFING

Earth is in desperate need of vital resources that have drifted into space. As the lone recovery specialist, your mission is critical: guide these precious resources safely back to Earth!

With precision and skill, you must calculate the perfect trajectory to push each resource past celestial bodies and through the void of space. Apply just the right angle and velocity to land them on Earth's surface.

Be warned: resources are lost forever if they crash into other planets or drift into the endless abyss of deep space.

Each mission consists of 20 resources waiting to be recovered. Earth's future depends on how many you can successfully deliver home!

Can you master the cosmic currents and become Earth's greatest resource recovery expert?
